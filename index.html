About Me

Updates: I am now working as an Associate Researcher at Beihang University. My new webpage can be found here.

I got my PhD in the Computer Science Department at UCLA in 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Professor Song-Chun Zhu. My research interests include Computer Vision and Big Data Analysis.
